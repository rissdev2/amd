FENDI X BENZEMA

The new Official FENDI Faster Ambassador, Benzema embodies the progressive spirit of the Maison's latest sneaker design – as a world-beating athlete with Real Madrid and recently UEFA Men’s Player of the Year, Karim is known as an exemplary player and a true professional, making the decorated striker an ideal ambassador for the FENDI Faster.
